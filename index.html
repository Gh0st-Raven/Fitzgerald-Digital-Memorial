Dear Mother
The absence of your presence has carved a profound void that words struggle to express. You were, unequivocally, the most extraordinary woman in the world. Your love, a steady anchor in an ocean of worry and uncertainty, now leaves a wound that time seems unable to heal. You embodied strength, kindness, and grace. Your laughter resonated through the home, a source of solace in life's complexities. Your wisdom was a guiding light, and your hugs offered comfort in moments of despair. Your home-cooked meals weren't just sustenance; they were a tangible expression of the love that defined you. The yearning to see you, to hear your voice, is an ever-present weight. Though your physical presence is gone, the impact of your love endures in the fabric of our lives. I cling to the memories we shared and strive to honor the lessons you imparted. Mom, the depth of my missing you is immeasurable, and your love remains an unwavering force in my life. One day, I will visit you in person once more, if only just to be able to feel closer to you than this ache in my heart.
